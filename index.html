What is DiversityCraft?

DiversityCraft is a community that was created back on the 4th February 2014 as a friendly and chill survival server with a few close people playing on it, since it's beginning the server never had any type of moderation or rules to follow. It's a free-for-all survival environment with the goal of letting the players play the game however they like, build whatever they have mind.
